Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical branding project take?

The timeline for branding projects varies depending on complexity. Simple logo designs can take 1 day, while comprehensive brand identity packages typically require 3-6 days. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you offer emergency or rush services?

Yes, we offer expedited services for urgent projects. Rush services typically incur an additional fee of 5-7% depending on the turnaround time required. Contact us directly to discuss your specific timeline needs.

What file formats do you provide for final designs?

We provide comprehensive file packages including vector files (AI, EPS), high-resolution raster files (PNG, JPG), and print-ready PDFs. We also provide guidelines on how to use your brand assets across different media.

Can you work with my existing brand guidelines?

Absolutely! We're experienced at working within existing brand guidelines to ensure consistency across all your materials. We can also help refresh or update your current brand guidelines if needed.

What is your revision policy?

We include 2-3 rounds of revisions in our standard packages to ensure your complete satisfaction. Additional revisions can be accommodated for an additional fee. We believe in collaboration and want to make sure you're thrilled with the final result.
